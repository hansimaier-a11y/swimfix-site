Send your video
MVP workflow: share your clip as an iCloud or Google Drive link, then send it via email with a few details.
Step 1 — Create a share link
  • iPhone: Photos → video → Share → “Copy iCloud Link”
  • Or Google Drive → Share → “Anyone with the link can view”
  • Clip length: 20–30 seconds (max 45s)
Step 2 — Email the details

Click the button below. It opens an email draft you just send.

Open email draft Back

Tip: Stripe sends a receipt email. Paste the receipt reference (or your payment email) into the message.

What to include
  • Payment email / receipt reference
  • Stroke(s): free/back/breast/fly
  • Your goal: e.g. “faster 100m”, “better breathing”, “triathlon swim”
  • Pool length: 25m / 50m
  • Video link
